About Me

Hello, thank you for being here! My name is Jocelyn and I’m a college student majoring in computer programming. I initially built this website for my Website and Design class, but I plan on keeping it up and updating it with new content as I continue to develop my skills and expand my portfolio. I have a passion for upcycling and creating unique items, which is what inspired me to start my shop. I also have a love for TV shows and movies, so I plan on starting a blog in the future to share my recommendations and reviews, which will be linked here as well. My hobbies include going on walks in nature, spending time with family and my cat, and of course, creating! My goal is to upcycle clothes consistently to reduce waste in the world and help the environment!

I work from home and I wouldn't be able to do it without the support of the sweetest girl in the world, Kyoshi (yes after ATLA !). My cat is my everything, she inspires me to create more and is the best companion I could ask for. I also have a wonderful family that supports me in all of my endeavors and encourages me to keep going when things get tough. I am a bit of a collector, my biggest collections consist of funko pops and deer figurines.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website and being interested in learning a little about me! Anyone and everyone of all ages, genders, races, and backgrounds are welcome here! I am always open to new friends and connections, so feel free to reach out to me through the contact page if you want to chat or have any questions about my work! I hope you find something that interests you here, and if not, check back often for new content!
