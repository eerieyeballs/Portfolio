Want a custom item?

Thank you for your interest in a custom item! Here is where and how you can request one:

Custom Item Request

  1. Gather specifications: Colors, Size, Design (Find inspo on pinterest if needed!)
  2. Type the custom item in the subject line. Ex: "Yellow Banana Cat Toy" (You will be emailing me)
  3. If this is a gift for someone or a special occasion please specify preferred delivery date. (Please note that processing time may vary.)
  4. Submit request with all details to corrinecustoms@gmail.com

Wait for confirmation and updates! I will get back to any request within 2-3 business days. If your request is confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email with pricing information. More intricate designs may take additional time and will require a deposit fee. A form will be created in the future for easier submission, but for now please email me with your request! I look forward to creating something special for you or your loved one!
